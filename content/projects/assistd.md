AssistD is a research project building on the ADELE health assistant platform to develop AI-powered voice assistants for dementia care.

- Period: 2024-present
- Collaborators: ALMA PHIL, TUM, TUM Rechts der Isar Hospital, Pixida

The project explores speech-based detection of cognitive decline, adaptive conversational AI tailored to patients' cognitive abilities, and intelligent health information systems that support communication and coordination among patients, caregivers, families, and medical professionals.
