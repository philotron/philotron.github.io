ALPHA-KI is a research project focused on developing a digital health assistant embedded in a smartwatch to support the care of chronically ill individuals.

- Period: 2021-2024
- Collaborators: ALMA PHIL, jambit, Vernaio

The system combines efficient health data management, real-time emergency detection, and intelligent conversational interaction in a voice-first wearable setting.
