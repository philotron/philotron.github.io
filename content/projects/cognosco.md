COGNOSCO is a research project focused on developing conversational search systems that facilitate exploratory navigation and discovery of scientific publications using scholarly knowledge graphs.

- Period: 2022-2024
- Collaborator: Springer Nature

The project investigates how graph-structured scientific content and dialogue interfaces can be combined to make complex research collections easier to explore.
