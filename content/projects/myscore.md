MyScore is a research project exploring how distributed ledger technology can enhance international credit mobility.

- Period: 2020-2021
- Collaborator: RWTH Aachen University

The work focused on prototyping and evaluating distributed-ledger-based approaches for cross-institutional credit recognition and academic credential exchange.
